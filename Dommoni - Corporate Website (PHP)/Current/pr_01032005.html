Dommoni Company Launch

Enterprise Software Company introduces itself to the marketplace today.

La Jolla, CA, January 3, 2005 - Dommoni, an Enterprise Software Company based out of La Jolla California has introduced itself to the marketplace today with the release of its initial product offering, RoundTable Collaboration Environment. Dommoni is dedicated to delivering quality software with the goal of enabling businesses to realize their goals and potential.

"Our primary goal is to offer innovative and creative software products that allow our customers to be more effective and efficient through their use" said Brian Abbott, the companies Founder and Chief Executive Officer. He continued to say, "We work to see that any business who adopts our products is in a better position both financially and in terms of their operations because they chose Dommoni."

The company will continue to release software for RoundTable that allows it to integrate with today's most popular productivity software and enterprise systems as well as develop additional products. "This is the beginning of a long path to dreaming up and building amazing new software that will enable businesses to gain a leadership position in their field over the coming months, years and decades ahead" said Abbott.

The company has been privately funded and organized under the laws of California.
